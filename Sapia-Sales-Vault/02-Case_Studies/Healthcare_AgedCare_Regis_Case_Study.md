## The Challenge
Aged care faces an industry-wide turnover crisis. Traditional hiring based on "gut-feel" interviews or resume screening missed quiet, empathetic talent and led to bad hires, early attrition, and recruiter burnout.

---

## The Solution
Regis introduced Sapia Chat Interviews for Carers, Food Services, and Registered Nurses. Automated scoring shortlisted applicants based on empathy and resilience. Deployed nationally across 74 homes in just 4 weeks.

---

## The Outcomes

| Metric | Before Sapia | After Sapia | Change |
|--------|-------------|------------|--------|
| General 157-day Turnover | 10.33% | 4.52% | 56% Reduction |
| RN (Registered Nurse) Churn | 14.93% | 6.76% | 54% Reduction |
| Recruiter Screening Time | Manual screening | 7,300 hours saved in 157 days | Equivalent to 4 FTEs |
| Zero-Performance Churn | High early terminations | Zero performance-related terminations | 100% Quality of Hire |
| Chat Completion Rate | N/A | 94% | Strong digital engagement |
| Diversity Data Disclosed | Low | 97% of 20.8k candidates | High candidate trust |
