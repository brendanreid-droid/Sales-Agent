## The Challenge
During COVID-19, Woolworths had to hire 27,000 team members in under 10 weeks to keep stores operational. Their manual, resume-heavy screening and video interview process was unscalable, leading to candidate drop-off and massive hiring manager bottlenecks.

---

## The Solution
Woolworths fully automated its first-stage screening with Sapia's Chat Interview. Automatically shortlisted candidates progress straight to asynchronous, non-timed Video Interviews (with no manager scheduling required). All assessments are integrated natively inside SAP SuccessFactors.

---
