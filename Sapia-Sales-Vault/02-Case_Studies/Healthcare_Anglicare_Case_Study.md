## The Challenge
Stretched recruitment teams and rising turnover costs caused Anglicare's time-to-offer to exceed 40 days, losing high-quality carers to faster competitors.

---

## The Solution
Automated the top-of-funnel screening with Sapia's Chat assessment.

---

## The Outcomes

| Metric | Before Sapia | After Sapia | Change |
|--------|-------------|------------|--------|
| Time-to-offer | 40+ Days | 14 Days | 65% Reduction |
| Employee Turnover | High | 63% reduction in turnover | Major retention gain |
| Candidate Pool | Baseline | 30% increase in applicant pool | Expanded talent reach |
| Candidate NPS | Low | 9/10 | Highly positive experience |
