## The Challenge
A team of 18 recruiters and support staff spent 80% of their work week manually screening candidates, driving up operational costs and delaying hires.

---

## The Solution
Deployed Sapia's Chat assessment to automate screening.

---

## The Outcomes

| Metric | Before Sapia | After Sapia | Change |
|--------|-------------|------------|--------|
| Monthly Recruiter Workload | 80% spent on screening | 1,213 hours saved in Month 1 | 67.4 hours saved per recruiter |
| Financial Savings | High Overhead | $25,000 saved in Month 1 | Direct cost reduction |
| Completion Rate | N/A | 84.0% | Strong engagement |
