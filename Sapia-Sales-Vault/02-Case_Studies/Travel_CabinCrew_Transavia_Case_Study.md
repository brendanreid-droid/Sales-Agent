# Case Study: Transavia — Travel & Cabin Crew
**Publication date:** July 2026
**Approved for external use:** Yes
**Can name customer:** Yes
**Product in use:** Sapia Chat Interview
**ATS/HRIS:** Standalone (in partnership with Randstad Belgium)

---

## The Customer
Transavia is part of the Air France-KLM Group, operating cabin crew recruitment campaigns across Europe.

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Sector | Aviation / Travel |
| Size | 3,000+ employees |
| Geography | Europe (Netherlands/Belgium) |
| ATS/HRIS | Standalone / Randstad |
| Hiring profile | High-volume cabin crew & cabin attendants |

---

## The Challenge
When launching in new markets, Transavia had to hire cabin crews from scratch. Manual CV screening did not scale and missed core soft skills like communication and customer hospitality essential for cabin crew.

---

## The Solution
Transavia scrapped CVs entirely and implemented Sapia's Chat Interview to assess candidates on real customer-service scenarios.

---

## The Outcomes

| Metric | Before Sapia | After Sapia | Change |
|--------|-------------|------------|--------|
| Campaign Completion Time | Ran into December | Wrapped weeks early (November) | Multi-week time savings |
| Recruiter Time Reclaimed | Baseline | 37 days saved over 4 months | 42% workload reduction |
| AI Recommendation Trust | N/A | 99% of hires were Yes/Maybe | Zero gut-feel hiring |
| Brand Recommend Rate | Baseline | 76% likely to recommend after apply | Elevated passenger brand |
| Candidate Satisfaction | Low | 9/10 | High candidate delight |
