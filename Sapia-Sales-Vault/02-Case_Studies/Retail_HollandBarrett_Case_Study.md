# Case Study: Holland & Barrett — Health & Wellness Retail
**Publication date:** July 2026
**Approved for external use:** Yes
**Can name customer:** Yes
**Product in use:** Sapia Chat Interview
**ATS/HRIS:** Integrated

---

## The Customer
Holland & Barrett is one of Europe's largest health and wellness retailers.

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Sector | Health & Wellness Retail |
| Size | 8,000+ employees |
| Geography | UK / Europe |
| ATS/HRIS | Integrated |
| Hiring profile | Frontline retail store associates |

---

## The Challenge
Faced high early store associate turnover and a lengthy 21-day manual CV screening process that delayed store staffing.

---

## The Solution
Implemented Sapia Chat assessment at the top of the funnel to assess candidates for retail fit.

---

## The Outcomes

| Metric | Before Sapia | After Sapia | Change |
|--------|-------------|------------|--------|
| Time-to-hire | 21 Days | 7 Days | 66% Reduction |
| Early Turnover / Churn | High Churn | 30% to 89% reduction in churn | Strong retention |
