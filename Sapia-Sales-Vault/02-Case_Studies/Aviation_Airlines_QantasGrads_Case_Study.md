## The Challenge
In 2025, Qantas received a record 4,500+ applications for its Graduate Program. Sifting through CVs was useless since graduates have minimal work history, making it difficult to find quality talent fairly without scaling the TA team.

---

## The Solution
Qantas utilized Sapia's Chat Interview to assess graduates on competencies and potential rather than work history, integrating explainable scoring blind to gender, ethnicity, and background.

---

## The Outcomes

| Metric | Before Sapia | After Sapia | Change |
|--------|-------------|------------|--------|
| Recruiter Screening Time | Manual screening | 1,831 hours saved in 2025 intake | Substantial time savings |
| Candidate Completion | N/A | 96.7% | High graduate engagement |
