| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|

---

## The Challenge
Starbucks Australia doubled its store openings, creating a massive hiring surge. Their lean recruitment team was overwhelmed by manual CV screening and spent half their day playing phone tag, resulting in a high barista churn rate.

---

## The Solution
Integrated Sapia's Chat Interview with SmartRecruiters in a matter of weeks. The tool automates barista screening and shortlists based on soft-skills profile and growth mindset.

---

## The Outcomes

| Metric | Before Sapia | After Sapia | Change |
|--------|-------------|------------|--------|
| Early Barista Turnover | 25% | 11% | 56% Reduction |
| Monthly Screening Time | Manual screening | 1,900 hours saved per month | Lean team doubled volume |
| Recruiter Time Reclaimed | Baseline | 2 days saved per week per rep | Focused on high-value work |
| Candidate Completion Rate | N/A | 89.5% | High engagement |
| Candidate Satisfaction | Under 7.0/10 | 9.1/10 (from 35k interviews) | Direct brand affinity |
