Kmart & Target Australia are two of the country's most trusted discount department brands, operating 450+ stores.

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Sector | Volume Department Store Retail |
| Size | 40,000+ employees |
| Geography | Australia / APAC |

| Hiring profile | Massive volume (300k+ apps annually) |

---

## The Challenge
With 300,000 annual applications and massive peaks before key trading seasons, manual CV screening was slow, inconsistent, and misaligned with their inclusive community brand values.

---

## The Solution
Implemented Sapia's Chat Interview integrated with SAP SuccessFactors across all 450+ stores in just six weeks. Implemented custom live recruiter dashboards to track funnel efficiency, diversity representation, and post-hire retention metrics.

---

## The Outcomes

| Metric | Before Sapia | After Sapia | Change |
|--------|-------------|------------|--------|
| Time-to-hire | 3-4 Weeks | 5-7 Days | 80% Reduction |
| Frontline Retention | High Attrition | "Yes" candidates stay 2.5x longer | Significant churn reduction |
| Candidate Completion | N/A | 73.8% | 79.6% complete in under 24hrs |
| Candidate Satisfaction | 6.5/10 | 9.1/10 (from 600k+ interviews) | Exceptional brand equity |
| Brand Advocacy | Baseline | 91% would recommend | Strong customer bridge |
