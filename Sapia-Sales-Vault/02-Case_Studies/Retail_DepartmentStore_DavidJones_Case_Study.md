## The Challenge
David Jones' recruiters spent 46% of their work week on initial manual CV screening and scheduling first-stage interviews. During seasonal peaks (Black Friday/Christmas), applications would surge 5-fold, forcing the team to rush screening or overlook quality candidates without relevant work history.

---

## The Solution
David Jones replaced resume screening and initial interviews with Sapia's mobile Chat Interview, integrated with PageUp. Candidates are assessed on soft skills and brand alignment, rather than CV history.

---
