# Case Study: LNER — Transportation & Rail
**Publication date:** July 2026
**Approved for external use:** Yes
**Can name customer:** Yes
**Product in use:** Sapia Chat Interview
**ATS/HRIS:** eArcu

---

## The Customer
London North Eastern Railway (LNER) is one of the UK's leading rail operators, connecting London and Scotland.

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Sector | Transportation / Rail Services |
| Size | 3,000+ employees |
| Geography | United Kingdom |
| ATS/HRIS | eArcu |
| Hiring profile | High-volume host & station staff |

---

## The Challenge
LNER's hiring process took 5 to 7 weeks, causing high candidate drop-off. Sifting through 400-600 applicants per role manually was inefficient and failed to attract a diverse candidate pool.

---

## The Solution
LNER integrated Sapia's Chat Interview with their eArcu ATS, removing CV uploads and video interview scheduling for on-train host and station staff roles.

---

## The Outcomes

| Metric | Before Sapia | After Sapia | Change |
|--------|-------------|------------|--------|
| Time-to-hire | 7 Weeks | 3 Weeks | 57% Reduction |
| Diversity representation | Underrepresented | 30% ethnic minority through all stages | Substantial D&I increase |
| Chat Completion Rate | N/A | 97% | Near-perfect completion |
| Candidate Satisfaction | Low | 9/10 | High candidate alignment |
