# Company Brief: Spotless (Spotless Group / Spotless Facility Services)
**Date:** 2026-07-15
**Territory:** APAC (ANZ)

### 1. Company Snapshot
- Stage: Not independently listed. Wholly owned subsidiary of ASX/NZX-listed Downer EDI Limited (ASX: DOW, NZX: DOW). Downer gained control via hostile takeover in 2017 and completed full ownership in October 2020. Ownership has not changed since; Downer's FY26 half-year filings confirm Facilities remains a core reporting segment. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spotless_Group_Holdings)
- Headcount: 36,000+ (Spotless workforce). Parent Downer Group employs approximately 50,000 people across ANZ. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spotless_Group_Holdings), [Downer careers](http://careers.downergroup.com/)
- Revenue Band: Spotless Group Holdings Limited (the local entity) reported approximately A$2.4 billion revenue in 2025. Downer Group FY26 guidance targets NPATA of A$295-315 million with 1H26 EBITA margin of 4.6%, described as the best in over a decade. [IBISWorld](https://www.ibisworld.com/australia/company/spotless-group-holdings-limited/555/), [Downer 1H26 ASX/NZX release, 19 Feb 2026](https://downergroup.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/4/2026/02/2026-02-19_4_Downer-1H26-ASX-announcement_FINAL.pdf)
- HQ Location: 549 St Kilda Road, Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. [Craft.co](https://craft.co/spotless-group/locations)
- Key Markets: Australia and New Zealand. Serves government, education, healthcare, aged care, and commercial property sectors across 1,000+ customers with 100+ integrated FM services (cleaning, security, catering, maintenance, asset management). [Downer/Spotless overview](https://www.spotless.com/about-us/)
