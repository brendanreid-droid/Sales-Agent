# Company Brief: Reliance Retail Limited
**Date:** 2026-07-15
**Territory:** APAC (India) — note: India is not explicitly listed as "ideal" or "acceptable" geography in the current ICP Master Profile (which names ANZ, English-speaking APAC, UK, Europe, North America). Flagging for icp-scorer to confirm geographic fit before prioritisation.

### 1. Company Snapshot
- Stage: Private subsidiary of Reliance Industries Limited (publicly listed parent, NSE/BSE: RELIANCE). Reliance Retail Ventures Limited (RRVL) is the holding entity and has taken multiple strategic/financial investors (see Funding History).
- Headcount: **Disputed, unconfirmed.** Sources vary widely: 31,729 (as of 1 Feb 2026, per Unify GTM), 58,939 (Dec 2025, per Revelio Labs), 58,975 for 2025 overall (a reported 13.4% decline from 2024, per Revelio Labs), 67,435 for 2024. No single authoritative figure could be verified in this pass; treat any headcount claim in outreach as approximate and unconfirmed.
- Revenue Band: ₹2,71,000 crore (~US$32B) for FY25 (year ended 31 March 2025) per Tracxn. FY26 revenue reported at the Reliance AGM (19 June 2026) as crossing ₹3.7 lakh crore (~US$44B), with store count hitting 20,000.
- HQ Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.
- Key Markets: India only (domestic). Omnichannel footprint spans grocery, fashion and lifestyle, electronics (Reliance Digital), and JioMart quick commerce.

### 2. Funding History
- Total Raised (RRVL, cumulative since 2020): approximately US$9.8B from strategic and financial investors, per Crunchbase/TechCrunch.
- Notable rounds: KKR invested ~US$755M in 2020 at a ~US$62B valuation; Qatar Investment Authority invested US$1B for a 0.99% stake in August 2023 at a US$100B valuation; Abu Dhabi Investment Authority invested ~US$600M for a 0.59% stake in October 2023; KKR added a further ~₹2,069.5 crore in a follow-on deal (date UNKNOWN from this pass).
- Lead/Notable Investors: KKR, Qatar Investment Authority, Abu Dhabi Investment Authority, Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund, General Atlantic, TPG, Silver Lake, GIC Singapore, Mubadala.
- Last Raise Date: UNKNOWN for any round after October 2023 within this search pass.

### 3. Recent News & Trigger Events (Last 90 Days)
- **19 June 2026 — Reliance AGM:** Executive Director Isha Ambani announced Reliance Retail crossed 20,000 stores in Q4 FY26 and that JioMart now operates India's largest quick commerce network, using 3,100+ physical stores as hyperlocal fulfilment nodes plus 600+ dedicated dark stores, covering 1,200+ cities and 5,100+ PIN codes. FY26 revenue reported at ~₹3.7 lakh crore.
- **Ongoing (reported January 2026) — Store-led quick commerce strategy:** JioMart average daily orders reported up 3.6x year-on-year, with Q4 daily orders up 300%+ YoY, driven by converting existing retail stores into last-mile delivery nodes rather than relying solely on dark stores.
- **Category expansion:** Quick commerce widened into non-grocery categories, 682 electronics stores (Reliance Digital) and 1,700+ fashion/lifestyle stores now offering 2-hour delivery.
- Read together, this is a live, large-scale frontline and delivery-role hiring surge layered on top of an already massive (20,000+ store) operating base, occurring at the same time as unresolved, conflicting headcount data that suggests workforce volatility (see Section 1).
