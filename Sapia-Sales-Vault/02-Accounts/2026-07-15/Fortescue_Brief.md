# Company Brief: Fortescue
**Date:** 2026-07-15
**Territory:** APAC (ANZ)

### 1. Company Snapshot
- Stage: Public (ASX: FMG)
- Headcount: approximately 16,000 (dispatch brief; company also cites employees across 28 countries and 98 nationalities) ([Building a diverse team, Fortescue](https://www.fortescue.com/en/sustainability/building-a-diverse-team), pulled 2026-07-15)
- Revenue Band: FY2025 revenue USD 15.5B (down 15% year on year), underlying EBITDA USD 7.94B, NPAT USD 3.4B, record shipments of 198.4Mt ([Capital Brief](https://www.capitalbrief.com/briefing/fortescue-reports-41-drop-in-full-year-profit-as-iron-ore-prices-slide-9d63a898-0510-4fef-9d40-62bca3569e8f/), pulled 2026-07-15)
- HQ Location: Perth, Western Australia
- Key Markets: Australia (Pilbara iron ore operations, Iron Bridge magnetite project), with growth/energy operations and offices spanning Argentina, Brazil, Chile, China, Dubai, Gabon, Germany, Kazakhstan, Morocco, Singapore, Spain, UK and USA ([Careers at Fortescue](https://careers.fortescue.com/en/jobs), pulled 2026-07-15)

### 3. Recent News & Trigger Events (Last 90 Days)
- Fortescue confirmed cutting roughly 700 jobs across the UK and Australia in its Fortescue Zero (green hydrogen/EV) division as it offshores electric haul truck motor and power train manufacturing to China and Germany, reprioritising R&D over in-house manufacturing ([The Nightly](https://thenightly.com.au/business/mining/fortescue-slashes-700-roles-but-andrew-forrest-says-its-not-a-sign-the-green-energy-arm-is-flailing--c-15386989), pulled 2026-07-15; [Renew Economy](https://reneweconomy.com.au/fortescue-cuts-green-hydrogen-jobs-as-it-pivots-back-to-rd-rather-than-manufacturing/), pulled 2026-07-15)
- This follows an earlier round of roughly 90 job cuts in the green hydrogen division (Gladstone PEM50 electrolyser project) announced May 2025 after the company flagged global cost headwinds ([Mining.com](https://www.mining.com/fortescue-cuts-700-jobs-slows-down-green-hydrogen-plans/), pulled 2026-07-15)
- Despite the Fortescue Zero/energy division cuts, core iron ore operations show no hiring freeze: careers.fortescue.com lists 53+ live openings as of 2026-07-15 across engineering, technical, operations, maintenance and safety roles, concentrated at Iron Bridge, Pilbara Exploration, Cloudbreak, Solomon, Karratha and Port Hedland, plus active apprenticeship, graduate and vacation programmes ([Careers at Fortescue](https://careers.fortescue.com/en/jobs), pulled 2026-07-15)

### 9. The ONE Sourcing / Buying Signal Worth Opening On
- Fortescue is running two workforce motions at once right now: cutting roughly 700 roles from its green energy arm while keeping 53+ live requisitions open across core Pilbara mining, engineering and technical roles, a live test of whether their TA function can redeploy and re-screen at volume without slowing core iron ore hiring ([Careers at Fortescue](https://careers.fortescue.com/en/jobs), pulled 2026-07-15; [The Nightly](https://thenightly.com.au/business/mining/fortescue-slashes-700-roles-but-andrew-forrest-says-its-not-a-sign-the-green-energy-arm-is-flailing--c-15386989), pulled 2026-07-15).

### 10. Suggested Opener Angle (1 Sentence)
- Given Fortescue is simultaneously working through hundreds of green-energy redundancies and 50+ live openings across Pilbara mining and technical roles, its TA team is likely stretched thin managing redeployment screening alongside new-hire volume, worth opening on how a consistent screening approach handles both motions without adding headcount.
