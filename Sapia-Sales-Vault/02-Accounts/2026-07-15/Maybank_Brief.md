### 6. 4-Layer Pain Points Mapping
- **Surface Pain:** At 43,372 employees and active graduate/apprentice intakes running multiple times a year (FSTEP, GMAP, June and July 2026 internship cohorts), high-volume entry-level and frontline screening is a recurring, resource-intensive cycle ([gradmalaysia.com](https://gradmalaysia.com/graduate-employers/maybank), pulled 2026-07-15).
- **Business Pain:** ROAR30 explicitly targets doubling the "next-gen" digital/data/IT/ESG-skilled proportion of the workforce from approximately 30% to 60% by 2030, meaning Maybank must screen and select for new competency profiles at scale while managing operating expense discipline (opex already down 5.3% YoY in 1Q FY26 despite rising tech spend) ([The Edge Malaysia](https://theedgemalaysia.com/node/789910); [Maybank 1Q FY26 release](https://www.maybank.com/en/news/2026/05/28/maybank-net-profit-rm2-48-billion-1q-fy2026.page), both pulled 2026-07-15).
- **Personal Pain:** UNKNOWN. No public statement from HR/TA leadership describing recruiter workload or burnout could be verified.
- **Career Pain:** As newly installed Group CHRO (October 2024) inside a five-year strategic plan with a hard, board-endorsed 2030 workforce-mix target, Mazhatulshima Mohd Zahid carries direct accountability for demonstrating measurable progress on the next-gen talent shift ([Maybank news release](https://www.maybank.com/en/news/2024/10/02.page), pulled 2026-07-15).

- Recurring theme: "future-ready, high-performing workforce" and "transformative mindset and culture" as a named strategic pillar of ROAR30 ([Fintech News Malaysia](https://fintechnews.my/56126/banking/maybank-roar30-strategy-rm10-billion-technology-investment/), pulled 2026-07-15).

### 9. The ONE Sourcing / Buying Signal Worth Opening On
- Maybank has a board-level, publicly quantified 2030 workforce target (next-gen skilled talent from approximately 30% to 60% of headcount) baked into a five-year, RM10 billion strategic plan launched January 2026, which means TA and HR must now select differently, not just faster, and that mandate sits squarely with a CHRO only 18 months into the role ([The Edge Malaysia](https://theedgemalaysia.com/node/789910), pulled 2026-07-15).

### 10. Suggested Opener Angle (1 Sentence)
- Given ROAR30's explicit target to double Maybank's next-gen, digitally-skilled workforce share by 2030, the Group Chief Human Capital Officer is likely under pressure to prove her screening process can reliably identify these competencies at graduate and frontline volume, not just resumes claiming them.
