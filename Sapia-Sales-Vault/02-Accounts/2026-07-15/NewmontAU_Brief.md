# Company Brief: Newmont Corporation (Australian Operations)
**Date:** 2026-07-15
**Territory:** APAC

### 1. Company Snapshot
- Stage: Public (NYSE: NEM; dual-listed ASX: NEM, TSX: NGT) [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Newmont)
- Headcount: Dispatch brief cites ~1,748 AU-specific employees (Boddington WA + Tanami NT). Independent public estimate from Revelio Labs workforce-intelligence data puts the broader "Newmont Australia" entity at 2,143 employees as of December 2025, up from 1,547 in 2023 (a 38.5% rise) — flagging this as a source discrepancy, treat 1,700–2,150 as the working range. [Revelio Labs](https://www.reveliolabs.com/companies/newmont-australia/employees/)
- Revenue Band: Newmont Australia Pty Ltd entity revenue approximately AUD 3.5 billion (2024/2025). [IBISWorld](https://www.ibisworld.com/australia/company/newmont-australia-pty-ltd/11179/) — Global parent Newmont Corporation FY2025 revenue USD 22.67 billion, net income USD 7.1 billion. [SignalX 10-K summary](https://signal-x.app/stock/newmont/10-k/2025)
- HQ Location: Newmont Australia Pty Ltd is based in Perth (Subiaco), WA; global parent HQ is Denver, Colorado, USA. [Newmont Operations & Projects — Australia](https://operations.newmont.com/australia/)
- Key Markets: Domestic AU gold production from Boddington (WA) and Tanami (NT); global parent also operates across the Americas, Africa and Papua New Guinea. [Newmont Operations & Projects — Australia](https://operations.newmont.com/australia/)

### 2. Funding History
- Not applicable. Newmont is a publicly listed company (NYSE: NEM) with no VC/PE funding history. FY2025 global revenue USD 22.67B, net income USD 7.1B, per SEC 10-K filed 19 February 2026. [SEC 10-K FY2025](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1164727/000116472726000010/nem-20251231.htm)

### 3. Recent News & Trigger Events (Last 90 Days)
- Tanami Expansion 2 (TE2): primary crusher commissioned and materials handling on track for completion by end of Q2 2026; project remains on schedule for commercial production in H2 2027, total capex USD 1.7–1.8B. [Newmont Investor Presentation, May 2026](https://s24.q4cdn.com/382246808/files/doc_presentations/2026/May/11/Newmont-Investor-Presentation-May-2026.pdf)
- Newmont reports USD 12.8B liquidity (incl. USD 8.8B cash) explicitly earmarked to fund growth projects including Tanami Expansion 2 and Cadia Panel Caves. [Newmont Investor Presentation, May 2026](https://s24.q4cdn.com/382246808/files/doc_presentations/2026/May/11/Newmont-Investor-Presentation-May-2026.pdf)
- Boddington (WA) is completing a waste-stripping campaign in 2026, positioning for ~30% production growth in 2027; Q1 2026 output was constrained by bushfire-damaged water infrastructure repaired through February 2026. [SEC Form 10-Q, Q1 2026](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/0001164727/000116472726000019/nem-20260331.htm)
- Newmont will release Q2 2026 results on 23 July 2026 — the first full-quarter report under new CEO Natascha Viljoen, who took the role 1 January 2026 as the company's first female CEO in its 104-year history. [Stocktitan](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/NEM/newmont-announces-second-quarter-2026-results-conference-jd2504ptc0xj.html), [Mining.com](https://www.mining.com/newmont-restructuring-impacted-16-of-workforce/)
- Context (slightly outside 90 days but directly relevant): Newmont completed "Project Catalyst," a restructuring affecting 16% of its global workforce, in November 2025 as part of Newcrest-integration cost-out — meaning AU people teams are ramping TE2 hiring with a leaner corporate cost mandate still in force. [Mining.com](https://www.mining.com/newmont-restructuring-impacted-16-of-workforce/)

### 6. 4-Layer Pain Points Mapping
- **Surface Pain:** High-volume specialist and FIFO recruitment for Boddington and Tanami (including the TE2 ramp) runs through generic job-board postings funnelled into a Phenom-branded career site and CV/EOI pipelines — no evidence found of structured, standardised first-round screening. [Newmont careers — Tanami EOI](https://jobs.newmont.com/job/Tanami-Expression-of-Interest-CLC-NT/953076800/)
- **Business Pain:** FIFO mining turnover runs 30–40% higher than residential mining operations nationally, compounding an acute sector-wide skills shortage (mining ranked the second most acute skills-shortage industry in Australia, 2022–2024). Newmont AU must ramp TE2 hiring toward H2 2027 production while absorbing the aftermath of a 16%-of-workforce global restructuring completed November 2025 — smaller teams, more urgent, harder-to-fill remote roles. [CSG Talent](https://www.csgtalent.com/insights/blog/australian-mining-talent-shortage-2026/), [Mining.com](https://www.mining.com/newmont-restructuring-impacted-16-of-workforce/)
- **Personal Pain:** Bronwyn Baker is roughly two years into a mandate to "drive Newmont's people strategy and support growth objectives" in the region, while the business is simultaneously advertising for a Director, People Operations, APAC — suggesting the regional people-ops leadership layer is stretched or being rebuilt at the same time TE2 ramps. [HR Today](https://hrtoday.in/bronwyn-baker-joins-newmont-corporation-as-head-of-people-newmont-australia/), [LinkedIn job listing](https://au.linkedin.com/jobs/view/director-people-operations-apac-at-newmont-australia-4146314487)
- **Career Pain:** New CEO Natascha Viljoen (effective 1 January 2026) is steering the business through a stated 2026 focus on "cost base and productivity" following the Newcrest integration cutback, even as USD 12.8B in liquidity is being deployed into growth capital for TE2 and Cadia Panel Caves. AU people leaders will be visibly measured on delivering the TE2 workforce ramp without growing TA headcount, in a new CEO's first full year. [Mining.com](https://www.mining.com/newmont-restructuring-impacted-16-of-workforce/), [Newmont Investor Presentation, May 2026](https://s24.q4cdn.com/382246808/files/doc_presentations/2026/May/11/Newmont-Investor-Presentation-May-2026.pdf)

### 9. The ONE Sourcing / Buying Signal Worth Opening On
- Tanami Expansion 2's primary crusher has just been commissioned, with materials handling on track for completion by end of Q2 2026 — keeping TE2 firmly on schedule for commercial production in H2 2027. This is a live, funded (part of a USD 12.8B liquidity position), multi-year specialist FIFO hiring ramp, arriving at a company that just cut 16% of its global workforce and is visibly recruiting for its own permanent Director of People Operations, APAC.
