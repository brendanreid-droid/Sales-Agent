## Target Personas

### Persona 1: CHRO / Chief People Officer / Head of People & Culture
**Decision Maker | Economic Buyer**
- Owns: People strategy, DEI outcomes, HR tech budget, voluntary turnover reduction
- Cares about: Board-level metrics (diversity, employee satisfaction, cost-per-hire, voluntary churn reduction), brand reputation, risk of bias claims, team morale
- Pain: CV screening is subjective, slow, and produces inconsistent outcomes. High employee attrition creates massive invisible costs (re-hiring, retraining) and recruiter burnout from constant backfilling.
- Message angle: Data-driven selection that measurably reduces turnover within 12 months. Strategic equity at scale. "What does your first-mile of hiring say about your values?"

### Persona 2: Head of Talent Acquisition / TA Director / Head of Business Partnering / Recruitment
**Champion | Influencer | Day-to-day user**
- Owns: Recruitment process, ATS, recruiter productivity, candidate experience, hiring manager relationships, business partner alignment, recruiter fatigue/burnout prevention
- Cares about: Time-to-fill, quality of shortlist, team throughput, candidate drop-off rates, business unit satisfaction, recruiter time optimization
- Pain: High application volumes overwhelming the team. Recruiters spending >60% of their week on CV screening, phone screening, and playing phone tag. Repetitive admin leads to recruiter fatigue/burnout, leaving no time for business partnering.
- Message angle: Operational scale and time optimization. "Reclaim >60% of your recruiters' week by automating screening and phone tag, allowing them to focus on high-value business partnering and critical tasks."

### Persona 3: Head of People Systems / People Systems Manager & Director (HR Tech Leader)
**Technical Evaluator / Enabler**
- Owns: HR tech stack, ATS/HRIS administration, system integrations, data compliance
- Cares about: Integration security, API stability, compliance, data privacy, work disruption during implementation
- Pain: Fragmented tools, high maintenance overhead, integration failures, systems that do not work with their core HRIS (e.g. Workday/SuccessFactors)
- Message angle: Seamless API integrations, native Workday/SuccessFactors connectors, SOC2 compliance, data security, zero manual data transfers.

### Persona 4: COO / Operations Director
**Stakeholder at operationally-driven organisations (retail, hospitality, logistics)**
- Owns: Operational headcount, frontline workforce, frontline retention
- Cares about: Speed to productivity, cost of vacancy, throughput during peak seasons, frontline staff churn and retraining impact
- Message angle: "Every week a role is unfilled costs you [X], and early-stage churn ruins store productivity. Sapia cuts time-to-shortlist to 24 hours while selecting candidates likely to stay longer."

### Persona 5: CEO / Managing Director / Founder
**Ultimate Decision Maker | Economic Buyer**
- Owns: Corporate performance, stock value/investor metrics, enterprise brand, EBITDA
- Cares about: Top-line growth, bottom-line efficiency, customer satisfaction, ESG/DEI commitments, risk mitigation, HR capital efficiency
- Pain: Talent shortages bottlenecking business expansion, high frontline turnover damaging customer experience/revenue and creating massive retraining waste, underutilised HR teams stuck in manual administrative tasks
- Message angle: Enterprise value, EBITDA protection, and process scaling. "A subjective hiring bottleneck is costing you 2% on your EBITDA, while voluntary churn drains cash. We save >60% of screening time and reduce attrition within 12 months."

## What Sapia Does NOT Do
*Important for agent accuracy — prevents hallucinated claims. Never make these claims in outreach or calls.*
- **Does not automate hiring decisions.** Sapia provides ranked insights and recommendations — humans always make the final call. Never position Sapia as a decision-making system.
- **Is not a black box.** Every score is transparent and explainable. Recruiters can see exactly which competencies drove a candidate's score and why. Sapia can articulate its reasoning in plain language at any time.
- **Does not ghost candidates.** 100% of candidates who complete a Sapia interview receive personalised, developmental feedback — regardless of the outcome. This is a core product commitment and a key differentiator for employer brand.
- **Does not use video or audio data.** Assessment is text-only — no facial recognition, voice analysis, or video required.
- **Does not replace human interviewers for final-stage assessment.** Sapia automates and augments the first-mile screening; human-led interviews remain essential for later stages.
