# Warm Engagement 2-Touch Sequence
**For:** Contacts who have opened or clicked a Sapia/HubSpot newsletter — and match the ICP
**Channel:** SalesLoft cadence
**Timing:** Touch 1 → T+5 days (if no reply)

---

## Context
This contact has already shown passive interest in Sapia's content. They didn't reach out, but they engaged. That is a warm signal. The email should feel like a natural, human continuation of their interest — not a stalker-y "I saw you clicked our email" moment.

The rule: **Use the newsletter topic as a conversation starter. Never mention that you tracked their email engagement.**

---

## Touch 1 — The Warm Opener
**Goal:** Turn a passive signal into a two-way conversation, referencing the newsletter topic as a natural hook.
**Word count:** ≤ 100 words
**Subject line approach:** Reference the newsletter topic or theme, not "our newsletter"

**How to find the hook:**
- Research Analyst will provide: which newsletter they engaged with, its topic/theme
- Use that topic as the starting point — not the fact that they opened it

**Template skeleton:**
```
Subject: [Something related to the newsletter topic — e.g., "Skills-based hiring in [sector]?"]

[First Name],

[Reference the newsletter topic as if it's a topic you're thinking about — naturally, 
not "I noticed you opened our newsletter about X." 
Instead: "We've been getting a lot of questions lately about [newsletter topic] — 
especially in [their sector]..."]

[One sentence connecting it to Sapia's angle / what you help with]

[Light CTA: "Curious whether it's something on your radar?" or "Worth a quick chat?"]

[Sender name]
```

**Example (if newsletter was about DEI hiring metrics):**
```
Subject: DEI hiring data at [Company]?

[First Name],

The conversation around skills-first hiring and what companies are actually measuring has been 
picking up lately — especially in retail and hospitality where volume makes it even harder 
to get right.

Curious if it's a topic you're thinking about at [Company].

Happy to share what we're seeing if useful — 15 minutes worth it?

[Sender name]
```

---

## Touch 2 — Light Follow-Up
**Goal:** One gentle follow-up. Different angle from Touch 1. Leave with dignity if no response.
**Timing:** 5 business days after Touch 1 (if no reply)
**Word count:** ≤ 70 words
**Subject line:** Different from Touch 1 — try a peer reference or outcome angle

**Template skeleton:**
```
Subject: [Company] + [Industry peer] — similar situation?

[First Name] — one more note.

[1 sentence: peer company reference in their sector doing something relevant with Sapia]

[CTA — same light ask]

No worries if the timing's off. Happy to reconnect whenever it makes more sense.

[Sender name]
```

---

## Sequence Rules
- Touch 1 should feel warm and curious, not salesy
- Never reference the HubSpot engagement data in the email — it's a signal for us, not a topic for them
- If the contact replies, immediately route to Reply Handler
- If no reply after Touch 2, close the sequence gracefully — do not roll into the standard 4-touch cold sequence without Sales Director approval
