## Notes for Copywriter Agent
Champion mover emails work when they feel like a genuine reconnection, not a sales call. Study how the transition was acknowledged — warmly and briefly. Note how the new company's context was woven in without it feeling like you researched them extensively. The best emails feel like a colleague reaching out, not a rep following a signal.
